AVOID THE SANDPIT (DICEY GOLF GAME)

Game Rules:

  • The course is 50 yards.
  • The course is split into three parts.
  • The fairway (50-21 yards)
  • the SANDPIT (20-10 yards)
  • the green (10-0 yards)

Win Conditions

Avoid the SANDPIT at all cost!

Tip: You want to roll as close to 21 as possible and vault over the sandpit!

Another tip: Read every line. It may provide clues to your success
